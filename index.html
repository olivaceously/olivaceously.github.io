Who I Am

Product Manager, CSPO, CSM

I'm a multi-discplinary Product Manager and technologist with a computer science degree from Tufts University living in Boulder, CO.

I believe in design thinking, customer-centric development, agile and lean startup methodologies. I specialize in software-as-a-service (Saas) products and have experience with cyber security, kernel code, APIs, and web apps.

And best of all, I am passionate about building experiences users love.

When not releasing something cool, I can be found out on the trails around Boulder climbing, hiking and backpacking with my dog Basil, mentoring aspiring product managers, or learning something new (most recently horseback riding!).

Where I Work

I am a Product Manager at VMware Carbon Black.

My main focus is expanding our B2B SaaS platform capabilities. Currently, I'm leading the charge to define, deliver and iterate on the front page of our platform: the Dashboard.

Previously, I was a Technical Product Manager and Product Owner conducting user research, writing user stories and priortizing the backlog for Audit and Remediation, a product that allows security practioners to query their entire fleet with ease using the open source framework, osquery (based on SQL). Prior to that, I was an associate Product Owner for our macOS Sensor Scrum team.

Alongside my day job, wherever I go I've found ways to mentor fellow colleagues, volunteer in and outside of work, and absolutely love teaching what I know to others.

How I Work

Data-Driven, Customer Centric, Empathetic

From Agile to Waterfall, PRDs to MVP to BRP (Big Room Planning) and everything in between, I've seen a lot of product development techniques in a short period of time. I know what works and what doesn't.

To build truly excellent products, I place more emphasis on delivering the right features and products the right way - using data-driven insights, having a customer-centric strategy and executing with empathy.
