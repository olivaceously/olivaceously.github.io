Who I Am

Born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri.
Student, programmer, leader and dancer.

I am a third-year undergraduate at Tufts University in Medford, Massachusetts and aspiring web and software developer. Before coming to college, I had never taken a programming class, but fell in love the first week of Intro to Computer Science . I am also pursuing a minor in Linguistics and a second major in Spanish. I hope to combine my love of languages with my passion for technology in the future.

When I'm not sleeping, coding or studying, I love to hike in the White Mountains in NH, travel the world, dance with my friends, watch Game of Thrones, bake bread, learn other languages (currently, Spanish and Italian), and go for walks with my Newfoundland, Hamish.
